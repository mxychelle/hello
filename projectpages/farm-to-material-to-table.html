'Make it Count' is a cookbook and research workshop designed in collaboration with Kidney Care UK. To better understand the daily struggles faced by patients suffering from Kidney disease, our team adhered to the strict dietary restrictions of the renal diet for seven days and documented our feelings, findings and frustrations.

Our experience was presented and shared at the Tate Exchange (19-20 May 2018) to the general public, nutritional experts, dieticians and kidney patients. The space was designed to reflect a homely dinner table to encourage discussion, and to provide a comfortable space for others to share their own experiences.




li-m is an experiential design studio that uses the senses to bring clients and their audience together in the present. We do this through workshops, installations and performative experiences.
