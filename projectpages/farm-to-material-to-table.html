Farm to Material to Table is a performative supper hosted at the fictional Finsbury Municipal Farm. Through a five course meal, guests are introduced to six bio materials that are made from agricultural waste and sustainable processes. Each course is inspired by the look, touch, feel and process of a material.




li-m is an experiential design studio that uses the senses to bring clients and their audience together in the present. We do this through workshops, installations and performative experiences.
